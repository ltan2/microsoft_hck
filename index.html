Who we are?

We are Team Zero :). This project was created as a submission to Microsoft Hackathon competition. We focus on addressing the issue of literacy for Africa, to address the need for the lack of resources and funding for schools. Read on to see how we are making an impact



Our Believes

The African region is a diverse part of the world, filled with millions of people,with hopes, visions,stories, talents and backgrounds. However, over 60% of the population in Sub-Saharan Africa of children age 15-17 years olds, are out of school. Force to grapple with harsh living situations and other issues like poverty and hunger, education is left aside. But, just like you and me, we believe everyone deserves an education regardless of their background,race or status. Therefore, the aim of this project is to give young people knowledge, tools and resources to share their stories,while also learning and earning to support their living conditions
